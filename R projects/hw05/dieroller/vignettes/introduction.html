Getting Started with dieroller

Ren Yi Wang

2018-04-27

Coin Tossing

The package "dieroller" is a minimal implementation for simulating rolling a die a given number of times, and to visualize the relative frequencies in such a series of rolls.

Creating a die

The first step is to create a "die" object with the function die():

mydie <- die()
mydie
#> object "die" 
#> 
#>   sides      prob stringAsFactors
#> 1     1 0.1666667           FALSE
#> 2     2 0.1666667           FALSE
#> 3     3 0.1666667           FALSE
#> 4     4 0.1666667           FALSE
#> 5     5 0.1666667           FALSE
#> 6     6 0.1666667           FALSE

By default, die() creates a fair die with standard sides one to six, and with probability values for each side of 1/6.

You can change the defatuls with arguments sides and prob:

loaded <- die(sides = 1:6, prob = c(0.075, 0.1, 0.125, 0.15, 0.20, 0.35))
loaded
#> object "die" 
#> 
#>   sides  prob stringAsFactors
#> 1     1 0.075           FALSE
#> 2     2 0.100           FALSE
#> 3     3 0.125           FALSE
#> 4     4 0.150           FALSE
#> 5     5 0.200           FALSE
#> 6     6 0.350           FALSE

Rolling a die

Once you have defined a "die" object, you can use the function roll() to roll a coin a given number of times. The output will be an object of class "roll", which will contain the vector of roll:

roll100 <- roll(mydie, times = 100)
roll100
#> object "roll" 
#> 
#> $rolls 
#>   [1] 2 6 3 5 6 1 3 3 4 2 6 5 3 4 5 1 3 6 1 6 4 2 3 1 5 3 2 3 6 3 4 3 3 6 1
#>  [36] 2 3 5 1 4 3 5 4 1 6 4 3 6 1 5 1 6 5 6 2 6 6 5 4 1 6 3 5 6 1 5 6 6 6 4
#>  [71] 1 6 2 5 1 6 6 5 1 4 6 5 3 6 6 4 3 2 4 6 5 5 1 3 6 1 1 2 4 4

The summary() function gives you a nice table of frequencies:

summary(roll100)
#> summary "roll" 
#> 
#>   side count prop
#> 1    1    17 0.17
#> 2    2     9 0.09
#> 3    3    18 0.18
#> 4    4    14 0.14
#> 5    5    16 0.16
#> 6    6    26 0.26

Plot of relative frequencies

You can use the function plot() to create a graohic with the relative frequencies of a series of coin tosses:

plot(roll100)
